Inhouse Logistics Facts

Inhouse is the Operational Wing of the Chattanooga Logistics Department. We are responsible for the movement of product from arrival at plant to line delivery.

Divisions of Inhouse:
TCC - Truck Control Center
3PL - Third Party Logistics Providers (JIS, Warehousing, Sequencing, Container Management)
Conveyance - VW/Aerotek Internal Logistics
LCC - Logistics Control Center (Leitstand)
Supplier Park
